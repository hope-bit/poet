About

My name is Hope Wilson and I am a 17 year-old in Maryland. I am an avid TV and movie watcher, music-listener, and poem-writer. I have always loved writing poetry in class, but the outbreak of school shootings across the U.S. became my biggest inspiration. I found that poetry allowed me to express my feelings of anxiety over these situations, and could provide motivation to pursue better regulations.
