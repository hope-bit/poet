Rabbit Hole

I fell down a hole
With no rabbit in sight.
No fairy tale ending,
Or wishes by night.

This house that I’ve dug
Is more than to hide.
I’m stuck by myself
With ghosts by my side.

Temporary life
Fits a temporary living.
This pit where I lay,
the gift that keeps giving.
