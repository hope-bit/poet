The word Love

Saying the word love,
Flies off my tongue like a dove.
Feels as if I rise above,
Maybe trying on a new glove.

I’ll strain my awaiting ear,
Listening for the words I so want to hear.
I can tell that it is almost near,
Coming to make my world less clear.

Except, what if I don’t mean what I say,
What if instead of words I found a new way?
A way for emotions to no longer stray,
And to keep the feelings tightly at bay.

I’ve yet to actually say the word,
They have beat me in the race of being heard.
It seems fast yet slow, a little absurd,
But it feels right, so I rest assured.
